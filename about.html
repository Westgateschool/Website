    OUR HISTORY

    Westgate Academy started in 2015 as a day care and kindergarten home which later was transformed into a Mixed Dat Nursery and Primary School and steadily progressing aiming at completing the primary cycle in near future.

    It was at first known as Standard Junior School and later changed to its current name "West Gate Academy" depicting its location within the trading centre where it is found.

    It started with 10 pupils of whom 6 were girls and 4 boys.
